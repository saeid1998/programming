If greater than zero years and less than six years output khordsal

If greater than six years and less than ten years, koodak output

If it is greater than ten years and less than 14 years, the output is nojavan

If older is equal to 14 and younger than 24, javan output

If greater than 24 years and less than 40 years, bozorgsal output

If the older one was equal to 40 years, the output of miansal
